Producer(s):

Producers Eligibility

Producer eligibility is based on screen credit. For Spirit Award nomination eligibility individuals must have received screen credit as ''producer'' or ''produced by.'' Persons with screen credits of ''executive producer,'' ''co-producer,'' ''associate producer,'' ''line producer,'' ''produced in association with,'' or any other credit shall not receive nominations.

An individual’s screen credit may not be altered in order to qualify or disqualify an individual for Spirit Awards eligibility. In the event of any dispute concerning credits, Film Independent reserves the right to make final judgment based on Producers Guild guidelines.

Films whose main exhibition after playing one of the approved film festivals was or will be on cable television may be required to provide additional information to determine appropriate producer credit.

Category Information & Selection

Category Selection for Nomination
  • The distributor and/or production company’s authorized representative submitting this form must choose the categories for which they would like the film to be considered by checking the appropriate boxes below. If a film is eligible within a category, but the category is not checked, the film will not be considered in that category.
  • If the film is entered in multiple categories, it will be considered in all categories in which it is entered. The final category(ies) for which a film may be nominated will be at the discretion of the nominating committee.
  • Films may be eligible in several categories. However, films will be nominated only once in each category type (i.e. a film cannot be nominated for both Best Feature and Best First Feature).
  • Except for the category of Best Foreign Film, all nominations go to American films, defined as either:
    1. Having at least one U.S. Citizen or Permanent Resident credited in two or more of the following categories of responsibility:writer, director, producer; OR
    2. The film is set in the United States and fully financed by a company whose principal office is in the U.S.

Please check all categories in which you are submitting your film.
Do not check a category box if your film is not eligible for that category.

For the purposes of this submission form, all fiction films are referred to as ''narrative''.

Submission Agreement

In submitting a film for Spirit Awards consideration, the film’s owners are deemed to have given to Film Independent, its successors and assigns, the right to choose excerpts from the film or supplemental printed materials at Film Independent’s sole discretion, for incorporation into the Spirit Awards telecast for the year in which the film is in competition. Additionally, if the film is nominated, you hereby grant Film Independent, its successors and assigns, the right to use stills and/or clips (not to exceed three minutes) from the film for use in connection with the Spirit Awards, including promotion, publicity and advertising of the Spirit Awards, and to promote Film Independent by any means whether in existence now or hereinafter developed, in perpetuity. Use of such clips or supplemental printed material shall be subject to clearance of any other parties that may be required.

Each nominee agrees that any film, tape recording, or supplemental printed material that is furnished to the Spirit Awards in connection with any entry may be retained by Film Independent for file, reference and archival purposes, and may be viewed partially or in its entirety for judging purposes.

Owners of nominated films are obligated, if requested, to submit to Film Independent a high definition digital videotape of the film. Film Independent will maintain such tapes under tight security and return them immediately following the Spirit Awards broadcast. In addition, you agree to provide all required materials and contact information for nominees (see Nomination Requirements).

By signing below you are certifying that you have the full power and authority to execute this agreement on behalf of the Production Company or Distributor responsible for the film. You also certify that all information on this submission form is accurate. If the information submitted is inaccurate, the film may be disqualified from consideration.
